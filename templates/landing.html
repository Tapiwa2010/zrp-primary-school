About Our School

ZRP Zimuto Camp Primary School is a premier educational institution committed to providing quality education in a disciplined environment. Our school caters to students from ECD A to Grade 7, with a focus on academic excellence and character development.

We pride ourselves on our dedicated teaching staff, modern facilities, and a comprehensive curriculum that prepares students for future success. Our school is part of the Zimbabwe Republic Police community, instilling values of discipline, integrity, and service.

With a 100% pass rate in national examinations, we consistently produce students who excel academically while developing into responsible citizens. Our holistic approach to education ensures that students develop not just academically, but also socially, emotionally, and physically.
